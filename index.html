COMPETE TOGETHER

IN CHRIST

Founded 2010. Our mission is to grow spiritually, mentally and physically using sports ministry serving all age groups of our Orthodox Church. Christ in Sports is a place where sports and faith come together. Each season offers leagues in various sports and spiritual competitions to encourage personal growth and fellowship.

Sports & Spiritual Leagues

The CIS league consists of FOUR sports leagues and ONE spiritual league. Teams compete to earn points in each league throughout the season.

CIS Camp

CIS hosts a summer camp each year where campers play games, listen to talks, and build meaningful fellowship. Through these activities, campers grow closer to God while enjoying a fun and spiritual retreat.
